The Story

Meet Hammed Gbolarumi, better known as MAVALI – a boundary-pushing Nigerian Afro-fusion artist and producer whose music tells stories that bridge cultures and generations. His unique sound is a rich tapestry woven from Afrobeats, Reggae, Hip-Hop, Fuji, and Dancehall, creating something entirely his own.

Music found MAVALI early. Growing up surrounded by cousins who were passionate music enthusiasts, he discovered his gift for songwriting at just 10 years old. Young Hammed would light up school events with performances that left audiences spellbound, showing glimpses of the artist he would become.

2020 marked MAVALI's professional debut when he signed with a Lagos-based independent label. Taking a bold step in 2022, he chose the path of independence, dropping his official debut two-track single "Nice & Sleek" – a move that would define his authentic artistic direction.

His latest single "Alhamdulillah" is currently making waves worldwide, capturing hearts and speakers across the globe with its infectious rhythm and soul-stirring lyrics.
